Eligibility Criteria

English Terms:
  • If the customer's age is less than 25 years, their application will not be processed, and a guarantee from a family member is mandatory.
  • The customer's permanent address and current residence must be in Peshawar.
  • If the customer's residence in Peshawar is rented and is more than 15 kilometers from the city center, they are not eligible.
  • The customer's permanent job or their own business must be located in Peshawar.
  • If the job or business is outside Peshawar, the customer must own a permanent residence in Peshawar and provide two separate guarantors who are also permanent residents of Peshawar.
  • Students cannot apply as a customer or act as a guarantor.
  • Females can apply as a customer if they hold a permanent position in a government, semi-government, banking, or private institution. However, a female cannot act as a guarantor.
  • Individuals with online businesses are eligible if they have a government-approved office and can provide business records for the last 6 months.

Ineligible Professions

English Terms:
  • Lawyers and members of the judiciary
  • Army and Police personnel
  • Doctors
  • Property Dealers
  • Goldsmiths (Sonar)
  • Marketing professionals

General Business Terms

English Terms:
  • Individuals with permanent employment (government, semi-government, or private) and owners of established businesses are eligible if they are permanent residents.
  • Businesspersons who do not have a permanent, established business are not eligible to apply.
